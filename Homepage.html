NEP 2020

E ducation is Fundamental for Acheiving full Human Potential, Developing an Eqitable & just Society, & promoting National development. Providing Universal Access to Quality Education is the key to India's continued ascent, & leadership on the Global Stage in terms of Economic Growth, Social Justice & Equality, Scientific advancement, National Intigration, & Cultural Preservation. The global Education Development Agenda Reflected in the goal 4 (SDG4) of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable development, Adapted by India in 2015-seeks to "Ensure Inclusive & Equitable Quality Education & Promote life long learning opportunities for all" by 2030.



This National Education Policy 2020 is the first education polic of the 21st century & aims to address the many growing developmental imperatives of our Country. This Policy proposes the revision & revaming of all aspects of the Education structure, including its regulation & governence, to create a new system that is aligned with the aspirational goals of 21st century education, Including SDG4, while building upon India's traditions & value systems.
